# ACL Roadmap

## Purpose

This roadmap defines the current build path for the Applied Consciousness Lab (ACL) ecosystem.

Its purpose is to distinguish between:

- canonical doctrine
- in-progress model development
- exploratory and testable theories
- future public infrastructure

This file is meant to reduce ambiguity and keep the ACL ecosystem organized as it expands.

---

# Current Status Categories

## Canonical
Concepts, models, and files that are considered governing and stable unless formally revised.

## In Progress
Work that is actively being developed and refined.

## Exploratory
Promising ideas and theories that require further research, testing, or clarification.

## Future Infrastructure
Planned additions that improve research usability, navigation, publication quality, or collaboration.

That distinction protects clarity, credibility, and future scholarship.
